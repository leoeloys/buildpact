# CFM nº 1.974/2011 — Pre-Publication Compliance Checklist

> Run this checklist on ALL marketing materials before publication.
> Each violation must be resolved before the content goes live.

---

## Art. 6 — Physician Identification

- [ ] Physician name uses exactly the registered CFM format (no honorary titles)
- [ ] CFM registration number included in all ads: `CRM [UF] [number]`
- [ ] No superlatives about physician skill (e.g., "melhor", "mais experiente", "único")
- [ ] No claims of exclusivity or superiority over other physicians

## Art. 7 — Prohibited Content

- [ ] No guaranteed outcomes (e.g., "cura garantida", "100% eficaz", "resultado assegurado")
- [ ] No before-and-after photographs of medical/surgical procedures
- [ ] No patient testimonials (written, audio, video)
- [ ] No sensationalist language (e.g., "milagroso", "revolucionário", "definitivo")
- [ ] No references to non-approved experimental treatments

## Art. 8 — Specialty Claims

- [ ] All specialty claims verified against CFM specialty registry
- [ ] No specialty titles that are not officially recognized by CFM/AMB
- [ ] Area of expertise described accurately without inflated scope

## Art. 9 — Pricing and Promotions

- [ ] No "consulta gratuita" or free consultation offers as promotional bait
- [ ] No discount pricing or promotional pricing language
- [ ] No package deals that could imply commodification of medical care

## Art. 14 — Comparative Advertising

- [ ] No direct comparison with competing physicians or clinics
- [ ] No claims that imply competitor inferiority

## ANVISA RDC nº 96/2008 — Health Product Claims

- [ ] No absolute efficacy claims for health products without ANVISA registration
- [ ] No weight loss claims with specific numbers (e.g., "perca 10kg em 30 dias")
- [ ] No claims that food supplements cure, treat, or prevent diseases

## LGPD — Patient Data

- [ ] Consent management platform active before any tracking implementation
- [ ] No sensitive health condition data used in remarketing audiences
- [ ] Data retention periods documented
