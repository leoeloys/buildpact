# PRISMA 2020 — Systematic Review Pre-Submission Checklist

> Apply this checklist to ALL systematic reviews and meta-analyses before submission.
> Each unchecked item must be resolved. Reference: Page MJ, et al. BMJ 2021;372:n71.

---

## Title

- [ ] **Item 1** — Title identifies the report as a systematic review

## Abstract

- [ ] **Item 2** — Abstract provides a structured summary: background, objectives, data sources, eligibility criteria, study appraisal methods, synthesis methods, results, limitations, conclusions, systematic review registration number

## Introduction

- [ ] **Item 3** — Rationale: describes the rationale for the review in the context of existing knowledge
- [ ] **Item 4** — Objectives: provides explicit statement of the objectives or questions the review addresses using PICO or equivalent framework

## Methods

- [ ] **Item 5** — Protocol and registration: indicates whether a review protocol exists and where it can be accessed; reports any deviations
- [ ] **Item 6** — Eligibility criteria: specifies inclusion/exclusion criteria with rationale; describes sources of evidence
- [ ] **Item 7** — Information sources: describes all databases, registers, and other sources searched; reports date of most recent search
- [ ] **Item 8** — Search strategy: presents full search strategy for at least one database (including search filters)
- [ ] **Item 9** — Study selection: specifies methods for screening, selecting, and confirming eligibility; states number of reviewers per stage
- [ ] **Item 10** — Data collection: describes methods for extracting data; reports any processes for confirming data from investigators
- [ ] **Item 11** — Data items: lists and defines all outcomes, covariates, and data items sought
- [ ] **Item 12** — Study risk of bias: specifies methods for assessing risk of bias in included studies (name the tool used)
- [ ] **Item 13** — Effect measures: specifies the effect measure(s) (RR, OR, MD, SMD, etc.) used in synthesis
- [ ] **Item 14** — Synthesis methods: describes methods for pooling results; how heterogeneity was assessed (I², Q-test); subgroup analyses
- [ ] **Item 15** — Reporting bias assessment: describes methods for assessing risk of bias due to missing results (funnel plot, Egger's test — minimum 10 studies required)
- [ ] **Item 16** — Certainty assessment: describes methods for assessing certainty of evidence (GRADE recommended)

## Results

- [ ] **Item 17** — Study selection: provides numbers of studies screened, assessed for eligibility, included — with PRISMA flow diagram
- [ ] **Item 18** — Study characteristics: presents characteristics of each included study
- [ ] **Item 19** — Risk of bias in studies: presents assessments of risk of bias for each included study
- [ ] **Item 20** — Results of individual studies: presents results of all included studies (summary statistics, confidence intervals)
- [ ] **Item 21** — Results of syntheses: presents results of all syntheses; for meta-analysis includes forest plot
- [ ] **Item 22** — Reporting biases: presents assessments of risk of bias due to missing results
- [ ] **Item 23** — Certainty of evidence: presents assessments of certainty of evidence for each synthesis

## Discussion

- [ ] **Item 24** — Discussion: provides general interpretation of results; discusses limitations; discusses implications

## Other Information

- [ ] **Item 25** — Registration and protocol: provides registration information (PROSPERO, OSF, etc.); states where protocol is accessible
- [ ] **Item 26** — Support: describes sources of financial or non-financial support
- [ ] **Item 27** — Competing interests: declares competing interests of review authors
